About Us

Like all good stories it starts off with a dream - a dream to make the world a healthier place

Our Story

We are four founders, each with our own unique journey, but united by a singular dream: to contribute meaningfully to the health sector. Our paths converged not in the playgrounds of youth, but in the corridors of academia and the forefront of technological research, where the seeds of a shared ambition took root.

Individually, we ventured down different avenues of medicine and technology, collecting experiences, insights, and expertise. Yet, a common theme resonated with each of us: the incredible possibilities at the intersection of healthcare and artificial intelligence.

Our collective journey began the day we decided that the future of healthcare could be different—more accessible, more intelligent, and more in tune with the needs of individuals. From that day forward, MyHealth became more than an idea; it became our commitment to you.
